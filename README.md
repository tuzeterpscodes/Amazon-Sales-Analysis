📊 Project Overview.

This comprehensive data analytics project evaluates global retail performance, operational efficiency, and consumer behavior dynamics for an Amazon-based e-commerce portfolio. By aggregating, cleaning, and modeling complex transactional logs, the analysis delivers executive-level visibility into market distribution, seasonal growth vectors, geographical demands, and promotional elasticity to drive strategic profit optimization.

💡 Key Business Insights Captured.

Market Share by Category: A strategic macro-assessment isolating total order volume distribution across core business units (Sports, Books, Beauty, Electronics, and Home & Kitchen), demonstrating portfolio stability and category equilibrium.

Business Drivers Correlation Matrix: An optimized lower-triangle statistical correlation analysis mapping numerical variables (Rating, Discount Percent, Quantity Sold, and Price) against Gross Revenue to isolate the exact drivers of volume velocity.

Temporal Patterns (Top 5 Sales Months): A deep seasonal profiling breakdown identifying high-conversion purchasing periods throughout the physical year, uncovering critical demand peaks (e.g., January and October) for inventory and supply chain forecasting.

Regional Density & Distribution: Utilizing advanced letter-value box plots to visualize logistical density across major global continents (North America, Asia, Europe, and Middle East), highlighting geographic performance baselines and transactional variance.

Geographical Matrix Heatmap: A high-density geographical grid evaluating sales intensity across explicit product-region pairings, isolating top-performing market intersections (such as Books in Europe and Beauty in Asia).

🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Demonstrated.

Data Processing & Feature Engineering: Python (Pandas, NumPy) — handling high-volume transaction arrays, statistical binning, and database structuring.

Advanced Visual Analytics: Matplotlib, Seaborn (Leveraging GridSpec multi-axis layout architecture, custom color palettes, and advanced statistical distribution geometry).

Corporate Dashboard Architecture: Implementing data-dense typography, consistent UI design principles, cohesive dark-mode formatting, and high-signal data visualizations tailored for stakeholders.
